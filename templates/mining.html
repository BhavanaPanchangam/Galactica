Microbial Mining

Uses bacteria or fungi to extract metals from asteroids.

Example: The BioRock experiment on the ISS.

Electrostatic Extraction

Uses electric charges to levitate and collect fine dust particles.

Laser Ablation

Vaporizes asteroid material into plasma to collect resources.

Bag and Capture Mining

Encloses an asteroid in a bag and slowly extracts materials.
